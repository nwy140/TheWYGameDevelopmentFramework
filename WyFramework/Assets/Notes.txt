
Drones flies around and pickup objects

Then drones drop objects at required location

Drones will destroy any obstacles by swinging the pickup objects at enemies 
or use the pickup objects to attach others 
or defend themselves

Drone will have to either
- Destroy all enemies
- Pickup and deliver all objects
- Reach Destination safely
- Survive within a specific time limit

In order to win the game!!!

Drone will release smoke after getting hit twice by an enemy 
Drone will break and become and destructible mesh when destroyed
